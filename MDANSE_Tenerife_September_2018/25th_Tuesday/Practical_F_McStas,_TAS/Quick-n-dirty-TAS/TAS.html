Description

Attempt to assemble a TAS in 10 minutes. Example is a mono rocking curve

Example: mcrun TAS.instr A1=9,11 A2=20 A3=-10 A4=-20 A5=10 A6=20 lambda=1.2 dlambda=0.2 -N21
